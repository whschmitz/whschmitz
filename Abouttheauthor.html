    About the Author

    My name is Will Schmitz and I am a junior studying economics at the University of Wisconsin- Madison. I have a great interest in current events, including politics, international relations, and government affairs. I aspire to educate and inform Wisconsinites about the candidates for this terms Supreme Court election! Misinformation and disinformation are all too prevalent in today's media. My aim as the author of this site is to better inform the public about politics in a truthful, unbiased manner.

    My Interests outside of Government:
    I enjoy exercising and playing sports, spending time with friends and family, and listening to my favorite podcasts.
    Where I came from:
    I was born in Milwaukee, WI, to two extremely hardworking parents who had careers in healthcare and public safety. Throughout my childhood, my parents were directly exposed to how certain policies can impact our communities, both positively and negatively.
